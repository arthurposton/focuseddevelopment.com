Software Quality Assurance Services Offered

We will provide our clients will all of the experiences that have been afforded us in our 13 plus year tenure in the Software Testing Arena. We will provide the professional services needed to fully test your applications thoroughly. We have a number of roles broken down for SQA within our organization. This will allow a company to pick the roles they need and not try and encompass everything in one role. The roles are explained below:

SQA Manual Tester -  This type of SQA role does redundant testing of the application. The person in this role does not need the skill set to create test cases or test plans or be able to follow them. This role is more of an entry-level role into SQA. This role is very good for a quick check of the system for about a month or so. *Note –Physical Application Tester

SQA Intermediate Tester -  This type of SQA role does redundant testing of the application as well as writing test cases and following a test pan. The person in this role does need the skill set to create test cases or test plans and be able to follow them. This role is more of an entry-level role into SQA. This role is very good for a quick check of the system for about a month or so. *Note –Physical Application Tester

SQA Team Lead - This role does manual testing as well creates test plans and test cases for the lower level SQA Tester and SQA Intermediate Tester. They are responsible for leading a testing project and ensuring that all aspects of it are on schedule. This role is more inline with an understanding of the SDLC (Software Development Life Cycle). They will know how to apply some forms of traceability in order to be accountable for the project they are working.

 SQA Automations Analyst - Using the preferred automation tool Quick Test Professional (QTP). Which is provided by the client. Automation test cases will be created programmatically that will allow an SQA Team to be more productive. SQA Automation Analysts create regression suites of test cases that can be run without the need of being attended to. With this in place SQA teams can be afforded the option to work on more pressing software needs within their projects.

SQA Automations Engineer - The SQA Automations Engineer will embody a lot of  the Automations Analyst duties but will also support the installation and setup of the tools to be used within the SQA Team. This includes the Quality Center Server with the defect tracking tool and installation of the Quick Test Professional tool while blending the two applications together. This role is responsible for setting up the users, and maintaining a working system for the SQA Team to use. This role Automates the SQA Team as a whole. The software needed to support this role will be provided by the client on their site and LAN.

SQA Senior Professional -  The Senior Professional role will lead the SQA Team from top to bottom. This person doesn't have to know how to do each role within the SQA Team they just have to be able to get the most out of the talent on the team. However they will have a great grasp of SQA and the SDLC. The person in this role is more of an executive and  has to keep the SQA Team as whole running as one.
