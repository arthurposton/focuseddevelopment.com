Computer Sales and Upgrades

We provide an affordable computer experience. We use a form that allows our customer to choose the components they want. We take that form and order the parts to build the PC you want at a great price. We are able to pass along some savings because we don't hold an inventory of merchandise. Our upgrades are performed in the same manner as our system builds we order when we find out what the problem is. We call  to let you know what was wrong with your PC and go over the options available for the part and then we order it. We are very thorough with builds and upgrades. We don't want you to have any problems with your PC after getting it back.
